# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

_Last updated: 2026-05-13_

## English

MCopy ("the App") is developed by Maoqi. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

**1. Data we collect**
MCopy does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. The App has no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers.

**2. Clipboard content**
MCopy reads from the system clipboard solely to provide its core feature — clipboard history. All captured clipboard content is stored **locally on your Mac only**. It is never sent over the network, never uploaded to any server, and never shared with any third party.

**3. Network access**
MCopy does not connect to the internet for any purpose.

**4. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions**
MCopy may offer optional subscription-based premium features in future releases. All subscription transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information. Apple may share with us limited, non-identifying information about your subscription status (e.g., active / cancelled) so that we can unlock premium features on your device. No personal identifiers are collected by us in this process.

**5. Children's privacy**
MCopy is not directed at children under 13. Since we collect no data, we cannot knowingly collect data from children.

**6. Changes to this policy**
We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

**7. Contact**
Questions? Email: lmq729981607@gmail.com

## 中文

MCopy（"本应用"）由 Maoqi 开发。我们非常重视您的隐私。本政策说明本应用如何处理您的信息。

**1. 我们收集的数据**
MCopy **不会**收集、传输或分享任何个人信息或使用数据。本应用不含任何分析工具、崩溃上报、广告 SDK 或第三方追踪器。

**2. 剪贴板内容**
MCopy 仅为实现其核心功能（剪贴板历史）而读取系统剪贴板。所有捕获的剪贴板内容**仅存储在您本地的 Mac 上**，不会通过网络发送、不会上传至任何服务器、也不会与任何第三方共享。

**3. 网络访问**
MCopy 不会因任何目的连接互联网。

**4. 应用内购买与订阅**
MCopy 未来版本可能会推出基于订阅的可选高级功能。所有订阅交易完全由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理，我们不会接收、保存或获取您的支付信息。Apple 可能会向我们提供有限的、不可识别个人身份的订阅状态信息（例如有效 / 已取消），以便我们在您的设备上解锁相应的高级功能。在此过程中我们不会收集任何个人身份标识。

**5. 儿童隐私**
MCopy 并非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计。由于我们不收集任何数据，我们也不会知晓收集到儿童数据。

**6. 政策变更**
本政策可能不时更新。更新内容将发布于本页面，并修改"最后更新"日期。

**7. 联系方式**
如有疑问，请发邮件至：lmq729981607@gmail.com
